    STRANDS

    STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

    Focus: Science and math-heavy subjects. Ideal for: Students aiming for courses like Engineering, Medicine, IT, Architecture, or any science-related field. Core subjects: Physics, Calculus, Biology, Chemistry, Computer Science.

    ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)

    Focus: Business and management concepts. Ideal for: Students who want to pursue Business Administration, Accountancy, Entrepreneurship, or Marketing. Core subjects: Economics, Accounting, Business Math, Marketing.

    HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

    Focus: Social sciences, humanities, and communication. Ideal for: Students interested in Law, Education, Psychology, Communication, or Social Work. Core subjects: Philosophy, Sociology, Anthropology, Creative Writing, Political Science.

    GAS (General Academic Strand)

    Focus: A flexible mix of subjects. Ideal for: Students undecided about their future course or wanting exposure to multiple fields. Core subjects: Combination of STEM, ABM, and HUMSS courses.

    Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track

    Focus: Skills for immediate employment or entrepreneurship. Offers strands like: ICT (Information and Communication Technology), Agri-Fishery Arts, Home Economics, Industrial Arts. Leads to TESDA certifications for skilled jobs.
