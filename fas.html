    FACILITIES

    Active Student Government (SSG)

    RThe school has its own Supreme Secondary Learners Government (SSG) that organizes events, represents the student body, and helps run school activities — giving students real leadership experience.

    School Culture of Leadership & Engagement

    Students regularly participate in intramurals, team events, and competitions that build teamwork and school spirit — like Mr. & Ms. Intramurals pageants and athletic competitions.

    Strong Senior High School Community

    The SHS department has its own online presence and community pages, showing how senior high strands and student life are celebrated and documented

    Focus on ICT and Research

    Rosales NHS has modern SHS‑level facilities designed for research, teaching, and learning enhancement, including dedicated spaces and programs that integrate technology use for students.

    A Campus That Attracts Students Beyond Rosales

    Students from neighboring towns like Umingan, Asingan, and even parts of Nueva Ecija choose to study here, which shows the school’s reputation and reach within the region.
